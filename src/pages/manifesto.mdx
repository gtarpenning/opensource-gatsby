# GNU Manifesto

[GNU Manifesto Source](https://www.gnu.org/gnu/manifesto.html)

The GNU Manifesto is Richard Stallman's call to arms for programmers and internet users alike. This document outlines his extensive his
extensive opinions on the topics of free-software, operating systems, and occasionally life itself. 

## "I am asking computer manufacturers for donations of machines and money. I'm asking individuals for donations of programs and work" -Stallman

1. GNU's Not Unix
2. Create a fully free and open OS
    - Editors, spreadsheets, documents, games, etc.
3. “Both C and Lisp will be available as system programming languages”
4. Timeline: 
    - Manifesto published 1985
    - All major OS components completed by 1990
    - Linus Torvalds releases Linux kernel in 1991-2
5. Spends most of the Document rebutting rebuttals:
    - Money
    - Creativity
    - Capitalism
